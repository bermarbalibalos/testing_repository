Job Description

HR Business Partner  

Role:

The role of the HRBP is to manage all HR issues across a number of accounts in APAC, providing HR consulting and coaching to the GSS in APAC, while also globally developing and managing some components of the overall HR strategy, policies and procedures.  The HRBP will provide best practices to managers in all areas of HR functions including Employee Relations, Compensation, Benefits, Employment Law, Performance Management, Employee Training and Separations.  The HRBP will manage a team of 2 (PHP and Indonesia) and will make sure to coordinate all regional HR efforts under the GSS APAC strategy. In addition, the HRBP will manage all “rebadge” activities in APAC.

 

Responsibilities

 

  • Overall and day-to-day business of planning and implementing HR plans and programs in support of the GSS objectives, ensuring quality solutions and deliverables.
  • Support GSS APAC management team by partnering closely with them.
  • Build and implement one consolidated  HR view for GSS APAC
  • Interface with CBG HRBP in the different account  and with HQ APAC
  • Serve as coach and partner in identifying, analyzing and resolving human resources and GSS APAC organizational issues.
  • Manage HR processes (performance, salary, bonus, onboarding, etc. across the region.
  • Work with managers to develop their skills in coaching, counseling and motivating employees.
  • Facilitate communication among employees and management, which may include 
    guiding managers and employees on problem solving and dispute resolution.
  • Work with Corporate HR to maintain policies and procedures that are consistent with legal and country guidelines.

 

Desired Skills & Experience

Requirements

 

  • 5-7+ years relevant work experience in a global/international service/consulting environment
  • Comfortable working in a dynamic, changing environment with the ability to juggle and prioritize a varied workload
  • Able to identify areas where new approaches would be appropriate; use initiative to generate, consult and implement new ideas
  • Strong customer facing skills (ability to work with a variety of personalities), ability to develop strong relationships, influence and motivate managers and employees, excellent oral and written communication skills, leadership skills, ability to work proactively and on own initiative without detailed guidance, at ease working with manager remotely.
  • Fluent both verbally and in writing in English
  • Computer literacy with competency in Microsoft Office package
  • Related further education is required with advanced degree preferred.
